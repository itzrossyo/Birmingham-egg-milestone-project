Who are we

Birmingham Egg Marketing was founded in the 1970's by Frank Hickton as a wholesale egg supplier selling eggs across the west midlands. In 1993 we introduced poultry, cooked meats, bacon, sausage and a selection of deli products. Even though we specialize in serving the wholesale sector, we are open to serve the public .Deliveries for large/regular orders can be arranged for local areas subject to conditions.Want to know more about our products/services ?
